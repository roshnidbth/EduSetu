Key Learning Outcomes

Upon completing this course, you will have the skills and confidence to tackle a wide range of algebraic problems.

  • Understand and use variables, expressions, and equations.
  • Solve linear and quadratic equations with ease.
  • Grasp the concept of functions and how to graph them.
  • Apply algebraic thinking to solve real-world problems.

Course Curriculum

This course is broken down into five core modules to build your skills step-by-step.

1. Introduction to Variables

2. Linear Equations

3. Quadratic Equations

4. Functions and Graphs

5. Polynomial Operations

Designed for Every Learner

We are committed to making education accessible. This course includes a suite of built-in tools to support your learning style.

Utilize features like Text-to-Speech for auditory learning, Adjustable Fonts and High-Contrast Mode for visual comfort, and Voice Commands for easy navigation. Our platform ensures that nothing stands in the way of your education.

Who is this course for?

This is a foundational course with no prior algebra knowledge required.

All you need is a solid understanding of basic arithmetic (addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division) and a willingness to learn. This course is perfect for middle school students, high school students, or anyone wanting to build their math skills.
